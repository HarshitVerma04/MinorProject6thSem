GPU Distribution (Final Optimized Plan)

GTX 1650 Laptop (4GB – Stable Models)

Trained:

ResNet18

DenseNet121

DMF-Net (ResNet18 + DenseNet121 Hybrid)

Why:

All three are CNN-based models

More stable on smaller GPUs

Manageable memory usage compared to transformer models

Good convolutional baselines for frequency-based learning

Suitable for 4GB VRAM environment

RTX 3050 Ti Laptop (Heavier / Transformer-Based Models)

Trained:

EfficientNet-B0

ViT-B/16

EfficientNet-B0 + ViT-B/16 Hybrid

Why:

ViT-B/16 is transformer-based and computationally heavy

Hybrid model combines two large backbones and requires higher memory

EfficientNet-B0 used as strong baseline on higher-capability GPU

Better CUDA cores and throughput on 3050 Ti improve training stability

Most computationally important experiments allocated to stronger GPU
