GPU Distribution and Training Environment

Hardware Used:

NVIDIA GTX 1650 (4GB VRAM)

NVIDIA RTX 3050

All models were trained individually on a single GPU (no multi-GPU training).

GPU Allocation:

A) GTX 1650:

1)ResNet18

2)MobileNetV2

3)DMFNet (ResNet18 + MobileNetV2 gated fusion)

B) RTX 3050:

1)EfficientNet-B0

2)ViT-B/16

3)Hybrid (EfficientNet-B0 + ViT-B/16 fusion)

Training Setup:

->Batch size: 8 (standardized across all models)

->Optimizer: Adam

->Scheduler: StepLR

->Deterministic training enabled (seed fixed to 42)

No mixed precision used

All experiments were conducted sequentially with consistent preprocessing and evaluation protocols.
