the workflow is as follows:


Phase1:
train model on real images(celebA datset) and fake ones(stable_diffusion dataset)
test on real+styelgan2_fake
these were all on raw rgb formats of the image


Phase 2:
generate feature images using filters for all 3(celebA real, stable_diffusionfake, style_ganfake)


phase 3:
train model on celebA real(rgb,feature image) and stable_diffusionfake(rgb+feature image)
test on celebAreal(rgb,feature image) and style_ganfake(rgb,feature image)


our objective:
see if prediction accuracy increases in phase 3 compared to phase 1.
