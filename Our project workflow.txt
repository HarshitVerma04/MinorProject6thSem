Project Workflow – Robust Detection of AI-Generated Faces

Phase 1 – RGB Baseline
We trained six models (ResNet18, MobileNetV2, EfficientNet-B0, ViT-B/16, DMFNet, Hybrid) on clean RGB face images (Real vs Stable Diffusion).
Generalization was evaluated on an unseen generator (StyleGAN).
Metrics used: Loss, Accuracy, AUC, Confusion Matrix.

Phase 2 – Frequency & Robustness Analysis

Phase 2A – High-Pass Training
We generated a high-pass filtered dataset to emphasize fine-grained artifacts.
All six models were trained and evaluated on high-pass images to study frequency-domain sensitivity.

Phase 2B – JPEG50 Robustness Testing
Without retraining, RGB-trained models from Phase 1 were evaluated on JPEG50-compressed unseen data.
This tested robustness under compression-induced distribution shift.

Final Experimental Scope
The study evaluates:

1)Clean RGB generalization

2)Frequency-domain behavior

3)Compression robustness

All experiments were conducted using standardized architectures, batch size, optimizer, and evaluation protocol.
